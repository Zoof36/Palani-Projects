The Story of Palani Projects

Our Beginning

Palani Projects was born from a deep love of craftsmanship and a profound respect our Hindu religion. For over 15 years, we have been creating handmade wooden products from our base in Durban, South Africa — each piece a labour of devotion.

What started as a passion project has grown into a trusted name for families seeking sacred items that carry genuine spiritual intention and uncompromising quality.

What We Create

Our specialty lies in handcrafted Hindu religious items — chariots, kavadies, bhangas, prayer items, decorative items, and more. We are not limited to religious work; we also craft custom wooden gifts, home décor, and keepsakes for any occasion.

Almost every product we make is a custom order. We take the time to understand your vision, consult the relevant religious texts and traditions where applicable, and produce something uniquely yours.

We also are not limited to the Hindu religion. We welcome and have worked with builds and people from all faiths, beliefs and cultures!

Rooted in Tradition

All our work is created within the bounds of Hindu religious laws and customs. Where sacred proportions, iconographic rules, or scriptural guidance apply, we research thoroughly and apply it faithfully. You can commission with confidence.

Our Promise

We stand behind our craft with a lifetime guarantee on all products. As long as your piece is cared for with respect, we guarantee it will endure. This isn't just a policy — it's our word, backed by 15 years of standing behind every item we have ever made.
